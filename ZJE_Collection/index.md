## 贡献指南 (Contribution Guide)

Thank you for considering contributing! Before you start, please read:

1. **Markdown files**: Fully supported. Ensure images are not local (uploaded images work automatically).
2. **PDF files**: Fully supported. The website compresses PDFs for download.
3. **Special formats**: OneNote, mind maps, etc. Upload to your cloud drive and share the link.
4. **Exam materials**: Not allowed (per school policy). Study notes and summaries are welcome.
5. **Questions?**: Contact me anytime!

See [Contributing](CONTRIBUTING.md) for detailed guidelines.
