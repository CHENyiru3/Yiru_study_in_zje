    IBMS总结复习 yicheng

    How the tutorial ask us to think...¶

    • 首先，Hypothesis/ Experimental purpose and question？
      • Model设计相关--> Model system是什么？Cells/ Animals/ human population? Why Do they chose the model? Are there ethic to be considered?
        • --> Group setting:
          • Negative control?
          • Positive control?
          • Internal Control for normalisation?
          • biological replicates?
          • How to control the variables/ factors?
      • --> Measurement approaches是什么？PCR, WB, IF, IHC, Sequencing....?
        • Are there technical replicates?
        • Why to choose this approach?
      • 结果相关--> Experimental output: image/ counts/ numbers
        • Data process?
        • Quantitative or qualitative?
        • Statistical approaches?
    • Pilot experiment: (Critical thinking part)
      • 是指在进行大规模试验前进行的先导的小规模的测试试验，验证可行性
      • Can the data be ==reproduced== independently?
      • Can they reproduce previous findings?
      • Awareness of potential bias?
      • Can the results be validated by an alternative approach?
      • How would you design it?

    实验设计原则¶

    • 这类题目可能是让我们解决一个生物学问题，需要我们设计实验流程和用到的技术
    1. 首先明确实验目的，核心问题¶
    • To determine/ validate ....
    2. 技术选择/技术的详细情况¶
    • 基因编辑和调控技术
      • CRISPR/Cas 9
      • CRISPRi/a
      • RNAi/siRNA
    • 分子生物学技术
      • Western Blot
      • Co-IP
    • 基因表达分析
      • Luciferase Assay
      • RT-qPCR
      • RNA-seq
      • Ribosome Profilling
    • 染色质与表观遗传学技术
      • Chromosome Conformation Capture (3C)
      • ATAC-seq
      • ChIP-seq
    • 蛋白质相互作用
      • PIP netork analysis
      • Cross-linking MS
    • 遗传关联和统计模型
      • GWAS
      • eQTL
      • Logistic regression model
    3. Control group setting¶
    • Positive control
      • 实验中使用已知会产生预期效果的样本或者条件
      • Including a known enhancer region as positive control
    • Negative control
      • 实验中使用不会产生目标效应（一般而言的）control group
    • Internal control
      • 内部对照是在同一个样本中测量的参考因素，用于标准化实验结果。
      • Normalize Western blot results using beta-actin as a loading control
    4. Results and analysis¶
    • What is the expected result? And how this can solve our problem
    5. Things to be noticed and optimization¶
    • What could be the limitation?
    • How can we improve?

    Question 3. [25 points] The integration of different omics to identify markers for a complex disease ultimately leads to the correlation of molecular events. In one of the simplest scenarios the outcome could be the correlation between two proteins.

    Suppose a proteomic study found that Kinase X highly correlated with the phosphorylation of protein B in your model tissue. Briefly, describe at least two experiments (in vitro and in cell) with their corresponding controls, that you would need to do to verify the observed correlation.

    • 分析：需要证明Kinase X和protein B phospohrylation的关系
      • 需要设计两个实验，in cell and in vitro
    • in vitro: 需要将纯化的Kinase X与protein B在含有ATP的缓冲液中混合孵育；
